# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 1 July 2026**

Focusy is built local-first. The short version: your data stays on your device,
we don't have an account system, and we don't run a server that collects it.
This page explains that in full.

## The short version

- **No account.** You don't sign up, and there's nothing to log in to.
- **No server storage.** Focusy has no backend. We never receive, store, or see
  your projects, tasks, or focus sessions.
- **No tracking.** No analytics, no advertising, no telemetry, no third-party
  trackers.
- **Your data lives in your browser** and stays there until you remove it.

## What Focusy stores, and where

Everything you create in Focusy is saved in your browser's `localStorage` on the
device you're using. That includes:

- **Projects** — names, colors, types, and their completion status.
- **Tasks** — titles, descriptions, pomodoro estimates, and progress.
- **Focus sessions** — start/pause times and your session history.
- **Settings** — your work-hour preferences (weekly/daily hours, lunch break).
- **Timer state** — the pomodoro timer's status and completed count.

This data is written to and read from your browser only. It is not transmitted to
us or to anyone else.

## What we don't collect

We don't collect names, email addresses, IP-based profiles, usage statistics, or
behavioral data through the app. Because there is no backend and no account,
there is simply no channel for that information to reach us.

## Browser notifications

If you allow notifications, Focusy uses your browser's built-in notification
system to tell you when a focus block or break ends. Granting permission happens
in your browser, and the notifications are generated locally. No notification
content is sent to us or any third party. You can revoke this permission at any
time in your browser settings.

## Hosting

The Focusy web app is served as static files. Like any website, the host that
delivers those files may keep standard technical logs (such as request time and
IP address) for security and operational purposes. These logs are a function of
web hosting, not of Focusy collecting data about you — the app itself sends no
information about your activity.

## Exporting and importing your data

Focusy lets you export your projects and tasks to a JSON file and import them
back later (see [Support](/docs/support)). These files are created and read
entirely on your device using your browser's file system. We never receive a
copy. Once a file is on your computer, keeping it safe is up to you.

## Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives only in your browser, **you** control its lifetime:

- Clearing your browser's site data for Focusy permanently deletes your projects,
  tasks, sessions, and settings.
- Data is tied to a specific browser on a specific device — it does not sync
  across devices.
- We cannot recover deleted data, because we never had a copy of it.

If you want a backup before clearing data or switching devices, export your
projects first.

## Children's privacy

Focusy is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children. As
it collects no personal data, it poses no data-collection risk to any user.

## Changes to this policy

If we change how Focusy handles data, we'll update this page and revise the "Last
updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take
effect.
